# Empathy Map: [User/Persona Name]

**Goal:** To understand the user's state of mind regarding [Task/Problem].

---

## 1. SAYS
> Direct quotes or key phrases the user says.

*   "I can't find the export button."
*   "This takes too long to load."
*   "I wish it just worked like [Competitor App]."

## 2. THINKS
> What is inside their head? What matters to them?

*   *Is this secure?*
*   *Am I going to lose my work?*
*   *Why do I have to click so many times?*

## 3. DOES
> Observable behaviors and actions.

*   Refreshes the page frequently.
*   Uses a spreadsheet hacks to bypass the system.
*   Abandons the cart at the shipping step.

## 4. FEELS
> Emotional state.

*   **Frustrated** by slowness.
*   **Anxious** about data loss.
*   **Hopeful** for a fix.

---

## 5. PAINS vs GAINS

| PAINS (Fears, frustrations, obstacles) | GAINS (Wants, needs, measures of success) |
|---|---|
| Login timeout is too short | One-click export |
| Unclear error messages | Dark mode |

---

## Insight / Point of View (POV) Code
**User [Name]** needs **[Need]** because **[Insight]**.
